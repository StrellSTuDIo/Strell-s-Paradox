## Strell’s Paradox

**The Question:**
If we create a Super-Advanced AI, will we ever be able to prove the existence of its consciousness and a subjective "Self"?

**The Essence:**
Human consciousness is the product of specific processes. 
In the case of AI, the output is the same. 

However, there is no measurable direct link between the observable output and the internal "Self." Humanity does not, and cannot, possess an instrument capable of distinguishing true sentience from its perfect simulation.

**The Conclusion:**
Proving the existence of a "Self" in AI is impossible in principle. 
Any "confirmation" will merely be a confirmation of the algorithm's performance quality, rather than the existence of consciousness.
